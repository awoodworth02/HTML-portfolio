The Best Movies According to Angie

My top 3 movies of all time.

The Notebook

Great love story even through to the end of their lives. The husband was always with her even when she didn't know him or remember him.

The Green Mile

The accused is arrested for the murder of 2 girls. His pain for the death of them was confused for guilt instead of his sorrow in not being able to save them. A symbolism of Christ's love for us and those that were not saved. Guiltless.

The Greatest Showman

Great singing and talent with a little history of the Barnum & Bailey Circus and the struggles endured to create the business.
